Who We Are &
What We Do
Welcome to Streams of Sound. We are a nonprofit corporation whose purpose and mission is to bring handpan music into healing facilities of all types. The handpan is a beautiful new musical instrument that has attracted sound healing practitioners around the globe.

Our Mission

Streams of Sound is a 501(c)3 nonprofit corporation, bringing handpan music into a number of medical/behavioral health facilities in Jacksonville, Florida. These facilities include hospitals, hospices, correctional facilities, and more. Our goal is to bring peace to the community through handpan music.


Our Journey

We believe strongly in the power of sound as an adjunct to healing, through stress reduction and relaxation. Having recognized the need for sound healing work in a variety of facilities in our community, we are poised to introduce the handpan instrument into our healing community.


Join Us

Uplift the health of our community together. Be part of the movement. We welcome new handpan musicians, handpan fans, and donations. Click on our “Join” button to find out how you can get involved or “Donate” to contribute to the cause. Bring peace to those who need it most.
